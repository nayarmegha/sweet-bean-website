Our Story

We are a Taiwanese cafe and bakery serving coffee, breakfast, pastries, and food located in Chicago's South Loop neighborhood.

We make Taiwanese-inspired pastries like Mooncakes, Taro Buns, Red Bean Buns and more! They are freshly made in-house and on daily rotation - you'll have something different to try everyday. We have ambient seating space - feel free to come by with friends, to work or to read as you enjoy our delicaies.

Location : Sweet Bean, 1152 S Wabash Ave, Chicago, IL 60605
